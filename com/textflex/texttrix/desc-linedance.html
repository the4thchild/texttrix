Line Dance
Remembers lines so that you can "dance" back to them as you navigate throughout your document. Line Dance serves as a navigational memory aid, without modifying your text at all.
  • Press the Remember line button to save the current position of the cursor.
  • If desired, click on the empty cell in the Name column to give the line a name.
  • Double-click on the line (or highlight it and press Line Dance) to go back to the saved line.
  • Press Forget line if you don't need to remember its position anymore.
  • Note: If you modify the document between the time when you remembered a line and when you return, the position may be slightly off.
