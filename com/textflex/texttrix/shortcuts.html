Shortcuts in Text Trix

Shortcut Types

Shortcuts are grouped into several different keybindings to reflect the more common shortcuts in various popular text editors.

  • Standard Keybindings. The shortcuts probably most of us are familiar with.
  • Hybrid Keybindings. A fresh blend of some Emacs single character shortcuts plus the standard ones.
  • Emacs Keybindings. Some of the more familiar single character Emacs shortcuts.
